Privacy Policy

How Gorilla ATM Security collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Gorilla ATM Security ("we," "us," or "our") operates gorillaatmsecurity.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you visit our website or contact us for a quote.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name and location
  • ATM type and deployment details
  • Any other information you include in your message

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, we automatically collect certain technical data through cookies and analytics tools, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (city/region level)
  • Device type

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests and inquiries
  • Follow up on your order or sales conversation
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Analyze traffic patterns to improve our marketing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Analytics and Third-Party Tools

We use the following services to understand site usage. Each service has its own privacy policy governing how data is handled:

  • Google Analytics & Google Tag Manager — tracks website traffic and user behavior. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — records session data and heatmaps to help us improve the user experience. Microsoft Privacy Statement

These tools may use cookies to function. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies — small text files stored on your browser — to support analytics and site functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and quote requests for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain our business records. Analytics data is retained per each tool's default retention settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
