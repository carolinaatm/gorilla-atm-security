Our Story

Built by Operators. For Operators.

Gorilla ATM Security was born in the Washington, D.C. metro area in 2013. Our founders weren't security manufacturers — they were independent ATM operators who watched their machines get hit. After a rash of break-ins that cost them thousands, they decided to build the enclosures they wished existed.

Today, with over 50 years of combined ATM industry experience on the team, we supply operators and merchants across the country with the toughest enclosures on the market — at a price that makes sense for independent operators.

We don't sell flimsy products dressed up to look tough. Every inch of steel we put in our enclosures has a purpose: to keep your cash in your machine and your investment protected.
